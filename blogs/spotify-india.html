Spotify India starts advertising on Televisions

Spotify India starts advertising on Televisions on Monday, making it the first entertainment company to establish an ad with a platform to reach people watching their shows on their TVs or entertainment center. The ad, starring Roar, just one of over 30 movies and TV shows slated to be shown on TV screens in the month of October, will be at the top of the homepage on Spotify's website on Monday. It will also be featured alongside the monthly top playlists on its Spotify catalogue in iTunes, Google Play and the Amazon Appstore. "The launch of this new Spotify campaign is very special for us because it is our very first foray into Indian TV advertising," says Rishi Pal, Vice President – World Regional of Spotify. "We are very excited to be able to contribute a creative and constructive feedback loop that is in sync with the ethos and culture of the campaign." According to the company, the multi-media play incorporates India's passion for cycling,, music and outdoor activities. "We wanted to create something relevant to this campaign in which an avid cyclist will be inspired to use their bike in exciting new ways to enjoy travelling," says Pal.
