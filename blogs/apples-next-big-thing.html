The series that follows the Apple Watch, and it will ship in October 2020 This series will feature an understated stainless steel finish with gold accents. We will first be looking at a sports watch in the Milanese Loop design and will see two strap options, a traditional strap or a newer, slightly more premium look. The design is reminiscent of the Basis Peak released by ETA in 2018, though this is very different. The Milanese Loop features a separate ring at the bottom of the bracelet to replace the standard strap that the Apple Watch comes with. There is also a rotatable crown on the side of the band to adjust the time and date, although you can use a flat screw to do so. The Milanese Loop is a great-option for those wanting a sporty look, particularly those who might have traditional wrist watch requirements.
