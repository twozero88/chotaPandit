How to monetize your new blog with adsense

How to get adsense approval for your new blogging website easily We get it. There is nothing better than having more traffic from your online adsense account and not having to care about branding any more. Blogs and new websites can easily qualify for top advertense ranking this way. Let us discuss briefly the steps on how to get the approval of your new website as a blog adsense account. Step 1: Go to your Adsense account, Click on the settings button. Step 2: Go to the SSL and select the default list of adsense servers. Step 3: Select the web hosts that you have to support and all of them will have a blue underline next to them. Step 3: On this screen you can enable free hosted ads, I personally don't recommend it but this is optional if you don't mind having to pay for ads. Step 4: If you use an adblocker, such as Adblock Plus, just block ads in that browser. Click Save That's it! Now all the following steps will take place when you go to your internet browser for the first time, a second visit will only show the websites that you currently want to see, and as soon as you leave those pages, the ads will vanish and your VPN service will start working like it should. If you've set up a PIN to your smartphone, go to the shop and take your battery out of the phone's memory. After that, plug the battery back in, and your PIN will now be activated (in case it had been deactivated before). What about the watch? The easiest way is to use your smartphone to record the time when your router is idle, and once the router is connected to your WiFi connection, record the time again when you're connected to the internet. Since the router only needs a couple of minutes to re-calibrate, your watch should now work normally. I've always recommended keeping your router data active as much as possible
